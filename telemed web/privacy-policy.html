Privacy Policy for Shastokotha

Welcome to Shastokotha

At Shastokotha, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your data is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard it when you use our website and mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Shastokotha Website and App, we may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

• Name: To identify you during medical consultations.

• Contact Information: Including your phone number and email address to communicate with you about appointments, consultations, or any updates.

• Medical History and Health Data: Information related to your health conditions, symptoms, prescriptions, and prior medical history, which you provide during consultations.

• BMDC Registration Numbers (Doctors only): We collect and display doctors' registration numbers to verify their credentials, ensuring they are MBBS-certified and registered with the Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council (BMDC).

b. Usage Data

• We collect information about how you use our services, such as device type, browser information, IP address, and interactions with the app.

c. Location Data

• With your permission, we may collect location data to provide localized medical services or direct you to nearby healthcare facilities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information in the following ways:

• Providing Medical Services: To offer free telemedicine consultations from licensed doctors.

• Communication: To send you information about your appointments, follow-ups, and medical advice.

• Personalization: To customize the services and recommendations based on your health data and medical history.

• Doctor Verification: To ensure transparency and compliance by verifying that all doctors are MBBS-certified and BMDC-registered.

3. Sharing of Information

Your personal information is never sold or rented. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

• Healthcare Providers: Your health data will be shared with the doctors providing consultations strictly for medical purposes.

• Legal Obligations: If required by law, we may disclose your information to government authorities or regulatory bodies.

• Third-Party Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers for hosting, data analysis, or communication services. They have limited access to your information solely for performing specific tasks on our behalf.

4. Data Security

Shastokotha takes appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use encryption and other security practices to ensure your data is secure.

However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

• Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal data at any time.

• Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent for data collection and usage. However, this may affect your ability to use certain features of the app.

• Data Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, except where we are required by law to retain it.

6. Children’s Privacy

Shastokotha is available for users of all ages. However, if you are under the age of 18, we require that you have the consent of a parent or legal guardian to use our services. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's use of the app to ensure that it is used responsibly and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

In case we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a user under 18 without proper parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Parents or guardians can contact us at any time to review, update, or request the deletion of their child’s information.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website or app may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date." Please check this page regularly for any updates.
